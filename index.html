Messages from this month.

This fun little yearbook project is a starting point of a more extensive initiative. Our aim is to catalyze an open collaboration of community-minded professionals who strive to improve the analytics behind their community-building/evangelism efforts.

At Keen IO, we devote tons of resources to community-building. Naturally, we want to understand the impact. There is data behind what we do; it's just difficult to gather and turn into meaningful insights. We want to tackle this challenge, and we know others do, too. So, we're inviting the community of community builders to collaborate with each other, share knowledge, tactics, and think together.

Sound vaguely interesting? It's all going down (for now) on Github. Join the conversation, help your peers, and learn from others' experiences.
